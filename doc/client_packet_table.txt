==========================================================================
 Packet Type Key
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

S -> Means a packet that will be sent from client.

R -> Means a packet that will be received by client.

B -> Means a byte.

w -> Means word (2 bytes)

l -> Means long word (4 bytes)

* -> Means repeat
